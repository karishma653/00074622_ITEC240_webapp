Mosquito-Borne Diseases

Mosquito-borne diseases such as Dengue, Zika and Chikungunya pose serious health risks to communities across Trinidad and Tobago.


Common Mosquito-Borne Illnesses

  • Dengue Fever – Can cause high fever, rash, and severe joint pain.
  • Zika Virus – Often mild, but dangerous for pregnant women due to risk of birth defects.
  • Chikungunya – Causes fever and severe joint pain that may persist for weeks.

Signs & Symptoms

  • Fever and chills
  • Rash or red spots
  • Muscle and joint pain
  • Headaches
  • Nausea and fatigue

How to Protect Yourself

  • Use mosquito repellent regularly
  • Sleep under mosquito nets
  • Install screens on windows and doors
  • Remove standing water near your home
  • Wear long-sleeved clothing outdoors
